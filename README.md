# Global Retail Sales & Profitability Analysis
### Tools: Excel | Python 

## Business Problem
Analyzed $12.6M in global retail sales across 7 markets and 17 sub-categories to identify profitability drivers and discount risk.

## Dataset
- Source: Superstore Global Sales Dataset
- Size: 51,290 orders
- Period: 2011-2014
- Markets: APAC, EU, US, LATAM, EMEA, Africa, Canada

## Key Findings
1. **Tables Crisis** — Tables loses $64K despite 
   $757K revenue due to 29% avg discounting
2. **EMEA Underperformance** — Lowest margin (5.4%) 
   caused by 20% discount — highest globally
3. **Discount Impact** — Regression (R²=0.772) proves 
   every 1% discount increase = 1.47% margin drop
4. **Growth Opportunity** — Canada has 26.6% margin 
   with zero discounting — biggest untapped market

## Recommendations
1. Cap Tables discount at 15% → +$135K profit improvement
2. Reduce EMEA discount to match EU (10%)
3. Expand Canada & Africa operations
4. Global discount policy: maximum 15%
